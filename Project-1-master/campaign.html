little boxes of happiness campaign

A final project for Graphic Design 2. The requirements were to create a campaign and develop its logo, poster and video advertisement. August 2015

When designing the logo, I focused on the main element of the campaign which is the gift box. I made many sketches of boxes, but then I moved to using the initials of the campaign name to create the box. This typeface was the best one to represent the edges of the box and the curves of the ribbon.

For the poster, I tried to show how this small box can connect different cultures together and bring joy to them.
